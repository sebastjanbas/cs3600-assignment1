About Me

Get to know who I am and what drives me.

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Sebastjan Bas, a passionate software developer with a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. I enjoy building meaningful digital experiences that solve real problems. Whether it's a polished user interface or a robust server-side system, I take pride in writing clean, maintainable, and efficient code.

I have experience working with a wide range of technologies including TypeScript, Java, Python, and SQL. I am comfortable working across the full stack and enjoy the challenge of integrating different systems together. I have built everything from real-time chat applications to language-learning platforms, always striving to deliver high-quality software.

Beyond coding, I am deeply interested in open-source contribution, software architecture, and continuous learning. I believe that technology has the power to connect people and create positive change in the world, and I am committed to being a part of that movement.

My Interests

  • Full-Stack Web Development
  • Application Design & Architecture
  • Open Source Contribution
  • Language Learning & Linguistics
  • DevOps & Cloud Infrastructure

My Background

I started my programming journey curious about how websites and apps are built. Over the years I have worked on a variety of academic and personal projects, gaining hands-on experience with modern frameworks like Next.js, Spring Boot, and React Native. I am currently studying Computer Science and continuously expanding my knowledge through coursework and self-directed learning.

One of my most proud accomplishments is building a Slovenian language-learning platform from the ground up, which includes personalized tutoring, scheduling, and on-demand coursework. The project gave me invaluable experience working with Docker, Grafana, Prometheus, and authentication systems.

I am always looking for new opportunities to grow, collaborate, and contribute to exciting projects. Feel free to browse my portfolio or get in touch if you'd like to work together.
