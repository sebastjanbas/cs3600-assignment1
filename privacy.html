Any personal information you provide via the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please be mindful of the information you share online.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us and we will respond within a reasonable time frame.
